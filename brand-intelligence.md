# Brand intelligence

## Employer recognition
- Target company: Deloitte
- Public sources: Deloitte corporate and careers properties
- Visible identity: typeset nominative `deloitte.` identifier paired immediately with `Independent candidate vision · Russell Dudek`
- The campaign does not represent, imitate, or imply an official Deloitte publication.

## Color provenance
- `#86BC25`: source-sampled Deloitte green used as the employer-recognition accent
- `#0A0A0A` and `#FFFFFF`: black-and-white editorial foundation consistent with Deloitte public digital presentation
- `#B7ED42`: candidate-original highlight used only for deployment-state motion and active controls

## Typography
Deloitte's proprietary typography is not copied or distributed. The implementation uses licensed/system-available Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, and a system monospace stack while reproducing the public typographic character through large editorial headlines, strong weight contrast, compact uppercase labels, and disciplined spacing.

## Role-derived visual system
The Enterprise AI Deployment Assembly is candidate-original. Context, tools, authority, evidence, observability, and ownership move into an assembled client-owned workflow. Motion explains the FDE mandate rather than decorating the page.

## Document continuity
The résumé, cover letter, interview brief, 90-day plan, contract, and PDFs use the same black, white, green, editorial rules, compact labels, and candidate-owned Deloitte role identification.
